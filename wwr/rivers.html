Wolf River

The Wolf River is the most prime white water in Wisconsin. It orginates above Pine Lake and flows for 240 Miles. The rapids rate anywehre from class 2 - class 4. This is for experienced whitewater adventurers only.

Rivers

The Wolf river, located in the northern portions of Wisconsin is the premiere site for whitewater lovers.

It features on of the best whitewater runs in Wiscosnin. The are several areas dedicated to intermediates.

Between Otter Slide Landing and Big Smoky Falls is the stretch that features class III and class IV rapids.

Camping

The largest campground of the Chequamegon-Nicolet National Forest is two miles northeast of the County M Landing at Markton

Boulder Lake Campground has 89 family-sites and 10 group sites in a gorgeous mixed-pine and hardwood forest next to a scenic lake. Most of the campsites are reservable.

Rapids

Farm Dam Landing is a wide and shallow start point followed by class III rapids.

A small clearing on both sides of the river is known as area 51 followed by several class III and class IV Rapids.

More Than Just The Adventure

This is a once in a lifetime chance to learn and adventure with experienced guides. The best rafting available in the country.
